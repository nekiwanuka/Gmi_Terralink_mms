# GMI Mining Management System - Usage Guide

This document provides a guide on how to use the GMI Mining Management System.

## 1. Introduction

The GMI Mining Management System is a comprehensive ERP solution designed to manage the various operations of a mining company. It includes modules for inventory management, sales, procurement, payroll, and mining-specific activities.

## 2. Core Modules

The system is divided into several core modules, each handling a specific business function:

*   **Dashboard**: Provides a high-level overview of the business with key performance indicators (KPIs).
*   **Inventory**: Manage your products, stock levels, locations, and transfers.
*   **Sales**: Create and manage customer invoices, payments, and receipts.
*   **Procurement**: Manage supplier purchase orders and track incoming stock.
*   **Mining**: Record daily mining activities, including labor and equipment costs.
*   **Payroll**: Manage employee payroll, including deductions and allowances.
*   **Contacts**: Manage your clients and suppliers.
*   **Reports**: Generate various reports for sales, expenses, and inventory.
*   **Settings**: Configure your business profile, currency, and other system settings.

## 3. Basic Workflows

This section describes the basic steps for common tasks in the system.

### 3.1. Creating a Sale

1.  **Navigate to Sales**: From the sidebar, click on "Sales" to go to the sales list page.
2.  **Create a New Invoice**: Click on the "New Invoice" button.
3.  **Select a Client**: Choose an existing client or create a new one.
4.  **Add Items**: Add items from your inventory to the invoice. Specify the quantity and price.
5.  **Save the Invoice**: Once all the details are entered, save the invoice.
6.  **Record Payment**: After the invoice is created, you can record a payment against it.

### 3.2. Adding a New Inventory Item

1.  **Navigate to Inventory**: From the sidebar, click on "Inventory".
2.  **Create a New Item**: Click on the "New Item" button.
3.  **Fill in Details**: Provide the item name, category, cost price, selling price, and other relevant details.
4.  **Save the Item**: Click "Save" to add the new item to your inventory.

### 3.3. Recording a Mining Entry

1.  **Navigate to Mining**: From the sidebar, click on "Mining".
2.  **Create a New Entry**: Click on "New Mining Entry".
3.  **Enter Details**: Fill in the details for the mining activity, including the date, site, and costs.
4.  **Save the Entry**: Save the entry to record the mining activity.

## 4. User Roles and Permissions

The system has different user roles with specific permissions:

*   **Admin**: Has full access to all modules and settings.
*   **Manager**: Can access most modules but has limited access to system settings.
*   **Staff**: Has limited access, typically to the sales and inventory modules.

This is a basic overview of how to use the system. For more detailed information on specific features, please refer to the relevant sections of the application.
